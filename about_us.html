About Electrico World

Air Distributors PRIVATE LIMITED and Aermec HVAC Engineering Co. started working in 2005 and become establish as a companies in 2014. From 2014 our companies are mainly focused on PAN india markets providing Supply and services for All Types of Air Conditioning.

In the initial years, the focus was to make a national HVAC Sales and Service company. There is a large requirement for HVAC with developers planning a National expansion and with the retail, mall and multiplex boom in its infancy, the need for a national Sales and Service Provider is in demand.

Consistent and repeat orders by many key clients enabled Our Companies to become India’s one of the leading HVAC Companies. Almost 65% of its order booking was coming from existing clients that include Tata Group, Eros international Pvt. Ltd, Tata Singapore Airlines, Mirza International Pvt. Ltd, Energy Gym, Afghan Embassy, Mount Carmel School and etc.

Our Companies are Air Conditioning and Refrigeration company, which providers air conditioning equipment’s of all leading Brands and has the experience and expertise to successfully complete all types of Commercial / Industrial projects, installation / commissioning and after sale service for the same.

Currently, the Company operates with a team of experienced engineer as well as installation and service technicians to service and maintain the products sold & installed by them.

A 24-hour service is available for clients such as Hospitals, Computer rooms, Industrial processes, Supermarket chains.>

Our Companies are active participator in industry training programs as part of its ongoing policy of staff training and development. Additional training is given to qualifying employees through suitable programs at companies and 'off campus’ technical expertise courses.
